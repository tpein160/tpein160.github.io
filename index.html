About me

I am a fourth-year PhD candidate with the Institute for IT Security at University of Luebeck, advised by Esfandiar Mohammadi. My main interests are differential privacy and machine learning. I want to improve the utility-privacy trade-offs of algorithms and enrich the understanding of data privacy and robustness in machine learning.

Outside of work, I enjoy spending time with my wife, working out and spending time outdoors with my dog.
